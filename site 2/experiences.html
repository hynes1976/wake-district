Our Experiences

Five ways to ride the wake on Lake Windermere — all welcoming to beginners.

Knee Boarding

Kneeboarding is the perfect mix of adrenaline and accessibility — fast-paced, fun, and easy to learn. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned rider, kneeboarding delivers smooth starts, sharp turns, and plenty of airtime just inches above the water. With a low centre of gravity and stable design, it's one of the most approachable water sports around, making it ideal for all ages and skill levels. From carving across glassy water to catching a little air off the wake, kneeboarding is all about confidence, control, and pure lake-day fun.

Wake Boarding

Wakeboarding blends the thrill of surfing, snowboarding, and water skiing into one unforgettable ride. Powered by the boat's wake, riders glide, carve, and launch into the air while skimming across the water on a single board. From smooth cruising to big airs and tricks, wakeboarding offers endless progression for beginners and experienced riders alike. With the wind in your face and the wake at your feet, it's all about balance, style, and pushing your limits—lake life at its finest.

Book Wake Boarding

Wake Surfing

Wakesurfing is all about endless rides and effortless flow. Using the boat's wake as a continuous wave, riders surf just behind the boat — no rope required — carving, cruising, and styling with total freedom. It's easy to learn, incredibly social, and perfect for riders of all ages and skill levels. From laid-back glides to sharp turns and tricks, wakesurfing delivers a relaxed yet exhilarating experience that captures the true spirit of life on the water.

Book Wake Surfing

Wake Foiling

Wake foiling delivers a smooth, almost surreal ride that feels like flying above the water. Using a hydrofoil mounted beneath the board, riders lift effortlessly above the wake, gliding in near-silence with unmatched control and flow. Once up, the foil creates endless momentum, allowing riders to carve, cruise, and float far beyond the wake with minimal pull. Whether you're chasing a calm, meditative glide or dialing in advanced turns and transitions, wake foiling offers a unique blend of innovation, balance, and pure freedom on the water.
