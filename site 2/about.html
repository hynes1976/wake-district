About Wake District

A family passion for the lake, turned into a business built around sharing it with you.

Wake District is a family-run watersports business based on the stunning Lake Windermere.

Having grown up spending our time on the water, watersports and lake days have always been part of our lives. What started as a personal passion has grown into a business built around sharing that experience with others.

What makes Wake District different is simple: we don't just run sessions, we share our passion. Every experience is personal, relaxed, and tailored to you — whether it's your first time on the water or you're looking to progress your skills.

We focus on creating unforgettable moments, building confidence, and helping people fall in love with the lake the same way we did. For us, it's not about ticking boxes or rushing sessions — it's about great vibes, genuine coaching, and making memories on Lake Windermere!
