Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before your day on the water. Still stuck? Get in touch.

What are the pick-up points?
We operate from The Swan Hotel, Lakeside & Fell Foot. Half-day and full-day sessions run from The Swan Hotel & Spa / Lakeside only.
Do I need prior experience?
No experience is needed at all! We welcome everyone — whether you're completely new, looking to refresh your skills, or keen to try something new. Our team will guide you every step of the way.
Is parking available?
Yes, parking is available at both Fell Foot and Lakeside.
What should I bring?
Please bring a swimsuit, towel, and weather-dependent items such as sun cream, sunglasses, or warm layers. Wetsuits can be provided if required.
Is there public transport?
Yes, there are bus stops at both Lakeside and Fell Foot.
Are there shower or changing facilities?
No, there are no showers or changing facilities.
Do you offer gift vouchers?
Not at the moment, but gift vouchers will be available very soon.
Do you accommodate non-English speakers?
Yes, absolutely. We use clear visual demonstrations and instructions so everyone can feel comfortable and confident.
